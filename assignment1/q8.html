Question 8

How does the interactive experience communicate how it should be interacted with over time?

Go Back
Upon first few interactions, the navigation is intuitive, and interesting. However, with interaction over time, I found that it is the animated visuals that makes it interesting. However, the navigation experience becomes lengthy with each animation and transition being so consistent and takes time to happen. I found it slow and slightly boring if I wanted to jump between sections, as I had to scroll through individual sections and wait for an animation or transition to occur. With that in mind, I find myself using the side menu more often for faster navigation, as it directly leads me to the page I want to visit.
